How Every Case Works

01
Intake & Scoping
No collection until the question, geography, risk level, and evidence threshold are defined.
02
Collection
Specialist agents collect from approved public sources only. Every tool is logged.
03
Preservation
All evidence is archived before analysis begins. Timestamps, hashes, provenance.
04
Verification
Every claim assessed against evidence. Confidence bands assigned. Gaps flagged.
05
Human Gate
QA, legal review, then mandatory human sign-off. Nothing ships without approval.
Evidence before assertion
We never claim more than the evidence supports. Every major finding carries a confidence band and a preserved source link.
Facts vs inferences, always separated
Confirmed facts and supported inferences are always labelled differently. Clients know exactly what is proven and what is inferred.
Lawful means only
Public records, licensed databases, open web. No hacking. No social engineering. No private account access. Ever.

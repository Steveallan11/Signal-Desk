# SIGNAL DESK — Company Brief
### A Lawful Public-Source Intelligence Agency on Paperclip

---

## Mission Statement

Signal Desk builds lawful, evidence-backed, auditable public-source reports by transforming approved
online sources into case dossiers, monitoring alerts, and due-diligence outputs — through a
CEO-led multi-agent workflow inside Paperclip.

The core discipline: every major claim in every final output must be linked to preserved evidence
and carry a confidence rating. Nothing ships without human sign-off.

---

## Company Goal (Paperclip Company Goal Field)

> "Run a lawful public-source intelligence desk. Accept structured case briefs, assign specialist
> agents to collect and verify public-source evidence, produce auditable reports with confidence
> ratings and preserved proof, and gate every external delivery behind QA, legal, and human approval.
> Never collect private data, never compromise accounts, never publish unreviewed claims about
> identifiable people or companies."

---

## What Signal Desk Does

Signal Desk is a commercial OSINT research desk. It is not a surveillance tool, a hack-for-hire,
or a stalking service. It is modelled on the discipline of investigative journalism organisations
like Bellingcat and the Centre for Information Resilience — but sold as a B2B service to:

- Law firms conducting corporate due diligence
- Compliance and sanctions teams mapping ownership structures
- Communications and PR teams monitoring brand narratives
- Journalists and newsrooms needing researcher support
- Public-interest bodies conducting accountability investigations

---

## Core Principles

1. **Lawful sources only** — public records, licensed databases, open web, published documents.
2. **Preserve before you analyse** — evidence is archived as soon as it is collected.
3. **Facts and inferences are always separated** — every finding carries a status label.
4. **Confidence is mandatory** — every claim has a confidence band (High / Medium / Low / Unresolved).
5. **No autonomous publication** — human approval is the final gate for all external outputs.
6. **Scope before collection** — no agent starts gathering until a case brief is approved.
7. **Privacy and safety checks** — every report is reviewed for harm before delivery.
8. **Auditability** — every action, tool used, and evidence item is logged with provenance.

## First Commercialisation Niche (MVP)

**Corporate Due Diligence & Ownership Mapping**

Rationale:
- Clear, defensible use case with established legal frameworks
- Clients have budget and compliance obligation
- Evidence requirements are well-understood
- Output format (due diligence memo) is standardised
- Risk profile is lower than people investigations
- Directly maps to Companies & Finance and Sanctions/PEP toolsets in the Bellingcat registry

MVP deliverable: a structured due diligence memo covering beneficial ownership,
sanctions exposure, adverse media, web/domain footprint, and key person flags —
with all claims linked to preserved evidence and confidence ratings.
